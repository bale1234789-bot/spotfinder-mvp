About SpotFinder

SpotFinder helps students, freelancers, and remote workers quickly find cafés and workspaces that match how they like to work.

Every spot is evaluated using real productivity metrics:

  • WiFi quality and stability
  • Seating & desk comfort
  • Noise level (quiet vs. busy)
  • Outlets availability
  • Temperature comfort

Our vision is to become the go-to layer on top of maps for “Where should I study or work right now?”
