SpotFinder

What is SpotFinder?

SpotFinder helps students, freelancers, and remote workers quickly find the best cafés and workspaces to study or work from – without wasting time visiting random places.

Every spot is evaluated using practical criteria that actually matter:

  • Seating & desk comfort
  • Noise level & focus friendliness
  • WiFi strength and reliability
  • Number of power outlets
  • Temperature & overall ambience

How we rank places

Our scoring model combines the spot’s metrics with your active filters. When you toggle filters (for example, “Quiet” and “Strong WiFi”), cafés that match those needs get a higher match percentage.
