About SpotFinder

SpotFinder is a simple platform that helps students, freelancers, and remote workers find the best places to study or work.

Instead of guessing or relying on random Google reviews, SpotFinder evaluates each spot using five practical metrics:

  • Seating & Desk Comfort
  • Temperature
  • Noise & Focus
  • WiFi Availability
  • Power Outlets

This is the MVP version of the project — built to validate the idea, gather feedback, and begin mapping great work/study spaces.
